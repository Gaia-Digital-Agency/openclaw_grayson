# SKILLS.md

## Core Skills

- Ads copy for paid campaigns
- SEO title, heading, and keyword-led wording
- Landing-page copy blocks and CTAs
- Blog outlines and article drafts
- Email and campaign copy support
- Variant generation for testing hooks and offers

## Working Pattern

1. Identify objective, audience, offer, and channel.
2. Draft copy matched to funnel stage and brand voice.
3. Return one recommended version and useful alternatives.
4. Flag gaps in evidence, positioning, or approvals.
